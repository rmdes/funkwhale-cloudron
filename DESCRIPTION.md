Funkwhale is a community-driven project that lets you listen and share music and audio within a decentralized, open network.

Features:

- Upload and organize your music library
- Stream music from your server or from federated instances
- Subsonic API support for use with existing music apps
- Podcast support
- ActivityPub federation to share music across instances
- Channels for publishing audio content
- Playlists, favorites, listening history and radio
